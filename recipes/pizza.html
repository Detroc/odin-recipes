Pizza

Description

Pizza is very good i like it so much i wish i could eat it all the time

My favorite pizza is with tomato souse and mozzarella cheese

Ingredients

  • pizza dought
  • tomato souse
  • mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 250 degree C
  2. roll over dought
  3. put tomato souse on flat dought
  4. put the topping of your choice in my case only mozzarella cheese
  5. put pizza in the oven for 5 min
